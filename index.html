Preparing for entry tests is overwhelming and expensive. StudyBuddy brings the notes, past papers, practice questions and video lectures you actually need into one organised place — for NUST, FAST, ECAT, NTS, PIEAS and USAT.

To make high-quality preparation material accessible to every student, and make the whole process more manageable and less stressful.
